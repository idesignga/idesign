Photography

The Pro Photography Problem

The most recent pillar in our New Business Owner bundle is offering professional in-the-field photography days for you and your team. These days, one of the greatest mistakes we see small business owners make is paying top dollar for a professional website, but then filling that website with cheap-looking content. Whether its the overuse of stock images or fake, AI-generated photos, the lack of any real images quickly takes away from a business’ credibility. On the flip side, many business owners attempt to present their own photos as a way to seem authentic (e.g., photos shot on their iPhones at the end of a job), but ultimately end up sacrificing quality and appearing unprofessional. Everyone knows that if you’re paying to have a website made, you ought to get professional photos taken; the difficult part is that hiring a photographer can be incredibly expensive. You could end up paying thousands for a single session and end up not liking any of the photos they develop. The question is: where else can business owners turn?
