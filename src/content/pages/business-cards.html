Business Cards

The Business Card Dilemma

Business cards are a strange niche among agencies and creative companies. A lot of people offer business cards on the side, and much like websites, you can actually get pretty far trying to make them yourself using online templates and web printing services. So why do the prices for getting custom business cards (and even custom logos for that matter) vary so much? The real answer is quite simple: value. New entrepreneurs are quick to overlook business cards as a simple means; just a quick way to share your contact information, so prospects can inquire with you further. Veteran business owners, however, understand that business cards can be your first major impression for any qualified lead—and first impressions count! So what should you do? Spend thousands with a dedicated designer or trust yourself to put together something great for less?
